Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use facial ID without 2FA?

No, facial ID must be used together with 2FA (TOTP) for maximum security. This provides two layers of authentication.

What if facial recognition fails?

If you can't enroll or facial login fails, you can still use traditional password + 2FA to access your account.

Can someone else access my account with a photo of me?

No! The system has live face detection that detects if you're a real person. Photos, videos, and masks won't work.

How many faces can I enroll?

Currently one primary face per admin. You can re-enroll anytime if your face changes.

Where is my facial data stored?

Only encrypted 128-dimensional face vectors are stored. The actual facial image is never saved.
